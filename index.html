Weekend in the Mountains

This weekend I traveled to the mountains with a couple of friends. The air was cool and fresh, and every path we walked led to a new hidden view. We spent the evenings sitting by a campfire, sharing stories under a sky full of stars.

One morning, we woke up before sunrise and hiked up to the highest point. Watching the first light spread across the valley was one of the most beautiful sights I’ve ever seen.
