Hawaii Travel Vlog

Day 1: Beaches and Sunset

We arrived at Oahu and headed straight to Sunset Beach. The golden sand and crashing waves made the perfect first day in Hawaii.

Day 2: Hiking and Nature

We hiked along the Na Pali Coast. The lush green cliffs and ocean views were breathtaking. Nature here is truly stunning!

  • Hike through the rainforest
  • Spot waterfalls
  • Take photos of scenic views

Day 3: Food Adventure

We explored local foods in Hawaii. Fresh pineapple, poke bowls, and shaved ice made our taste buds very happy.

  • Eat fresh pineapple
  • Try poke bowls
  • Enjoy shaved ice
